Episode 1, Part 1: The first podcast

Jersey Jerughaus is a podcast in which a group of pals meets at a suburban Jersey home which serves as a safe haven for the consumption of jerugs and the ensuing conversation and mayhem. The first episode is dominated by a substance nicknamed the Wonder Jerug II. With all the great times had, listeners should not overlook the fact that one hoarse-voiced participant is nearly destroyed by a high fever in this episode, induced by this substance which should never be tried outside the jerughaus. In the first part of the episode, the jerughaus experience begins. In the second part, coming soon, the psychedelia peaks and culminates in a strange musical experience.
